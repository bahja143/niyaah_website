About Us

Dedicated to Your Mental Wellness

Niyaah Digital Health Care is a pioneering mental health organization dedicated to supporting the emotional and psychological well-being of young adults across Somalia, with a special focus on underserved and marginalized communities. We believe that access to quality mental health care is a fundamental right and not a privilege.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, inclusive, and youth-centered mental health services through digital platforms, community engagement, and professional support systems.

Featured

Our Vision

To cultivate a mentally resilient generation of young adults empowered to live fulfilling and emotionally balanced lives.

Our Approach

We combine traditional Somali healing wisdom with modern therapeutic techniques. Our bilingual therapists understand the unique challenges faced by our community and provide culturally appropriate interventions.

Comprehensive Mental Health Care

We offer a wide range of mental health services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our community. From individual therapy to group sessions, we're here to support your journey to wellness.

Popular

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions with experienced therapists to address personal challenges, trauma, anxiety, depression, and other mental health concerns in a safe, confidential environment.

  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Bilingual sessions (Somali/English)
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Evidence-based approaches
$30 /session

Family Therapy

Strengthen family bonds and improve communication through guided sessions that address family dynamics, conflicts, and relationship issues with cultural sensitivity.

  • Family system approach
  • Conflict resolution
  • Communication skills
  • Cultural integration
  • Extended family support
$40 /session

Group Therapy

Connect with others facing similar challenges in supportive group settings. Share experiences, learn coping strategies, and build a community of support.

  • Peer support network
  • Shared experiences
  • Cost-effective option
  • Various focus groups
$50 /session
